About P.V. Sindhu

Pusarla Venkata Sindhu is an Indian professional badminton player. Over the course of her career, she has won medals at multiple tournaments including a gold at the 2019 World Championships. She is the first Indian to become the Badminton World Champion and the first Indian woman to earn an Olympic silver medal. She rose to a career high World ranking of no. 2 in April 2017.
